THE BADDEST BATCH IS BACK

FALL IN TROOPER!

Star Wars: The Bad Batch returns for Season 2! Months have passed since the events on Kamino, and the Bad Batch continue their journey navigating the Empire after the fall of the Republic. They will cross paths with friends and foes, both new and familiar, as they take on a variety of thrilling mercenary missions that will take them to unexpected and dangerous new places.

Star Wars: The Bad Batch Season 2 stars Dee Bradley Baker as the voice of the Bad Batch and Michelle Ang as the voice of Omega. Rhea Perlman returns to guest star as Cid, Noshir Dalal returns to guest star as Vice Admiral Rampart, and Wanda Sykes makes her guest starring debut in the series as Phee Genoa.

Watch Star Wars: The Bad Batch as it returns exclusively to Disney+ on January 4, 2023!

2 YEARS AGO...

On this day two years ago, the first two issues of the Star Wars: The High Republic comic series went on sale, beginning with the books "Light of the Jedi" and "A Test of Courage".

The release of these two books triggered a so-called "First Wave" of books that began to appear on the subject. We are set some 200 years before the so-called "Skywalker Saga", we are introduced to new characters and a period of peace that was not going to last too long...

FAN OF OPEN WORLD GAMES?

A few months ago we learned of the existence of a future video game developed by Massive, a Ubisoft studio.

The game, according to leaks, will be an open world in which you can travel between planets, create your own character and create your own story.

In recent weeks, some of the game's developers have posted public invitations on Twitter for anyone to sign up to test beta versions of this early-development game.

THE MANDALORIAN

March 1, 2023 is the date chosen for the return to the screens of Din Djarin and Grogu.

The last time we saw them was in The Book of Boba Fett.

Grogu and Din reunited after a harsh sendoff in The Mandalorian Season Two.

Apparently, Grogu chose to return to the mandalorian over staying with Luke Skywalker and training, will we see him return to him this season? Will they find another Jedi?

On the other hand, everything indicates that Din and Bo-Katan Kryze will find themselves on opposite sides due to who is the rightful wielder of the Dark Saber, what do you expect to see?
